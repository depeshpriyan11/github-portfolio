Who I Am

Hi, my name is Depesh. I created FitForward because I noticed that a lot of beginners feel overwhelmed when trying to start fitness. Most online advice is either too advanced or unrealistic, which makes people give up early.

I enjoy fitness, sports, and building routines that actually fit into a busy student lifestyle. My goal with this website is to make fitness feel simple, achievable, and not intimidating for beginners.

FitForward is built around the idea that consistency matters more than intensity, and anyone can improve their health with the right structure and mindset.
