Fuel Your Body

Nutrition is one of the most important parts of fitness, but it is often misunderstood. You don’t need strict diets or expensive meal plans to see results.

Instead, beginners should focus on balance, consistency, and basic awareness of what they eat.

Protein

Supports muscle recovery and growth after workouts.

Carbs

Provide energy for workouts and daily activity.

Hydration

Improves performance and helps recovery.

Simple Nutrition Rules

1. Eat balanced meals instead of restricting food completely.

2. Drink enough water throughout the day.

3. Focus on consistency instead of perfection.
