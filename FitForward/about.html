My Story

My name is Depesh, and I created FitForward because I noticed a common problem among beginners: most people want to get fit, but they don’t know where to start.

When I first started learning about fitness, I saw that a lot of content online was either too advanced, too complicated, or unrealistic for someone with a busy student lifestyle.

That made fitness feel intimidating instead of helpful. FitForward is my solution to that problem.

My Goal

Make fitness simple and accessible for beginners.

My Approach

Focus on consistency instead of intensity.

My Vision

Help people build lifelong healthy habits.

What I Learned

Through fitness and personal experience, I learned that progress is not about perfection — it’s about showing up even when motivation is low.

That mindset is what FitForward is built on.
